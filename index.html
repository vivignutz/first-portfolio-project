Who makes this Podcast?

PodNagringa's mission

The mission of this podcast is to inspire people from all over Brazil who want to move abroad, either to work, live or even for a sabbatical year, through real experiences told by real people who have already had their journey one day.


Through these inspiring stories, you will be captivated by the world of possibilities that will appear in your mind, so that you can program yourself to fulfil your dream of living other experiences in other countries.

Who I am

Hi! I'm Vivi, from Berlin, Germany!


After a some time planning, I am very glad to start this project that was already shelved for a while due to unforeseeable circumstances. It's a huge pleasure to get Brazilians who already arrived in Europe and built their own dreams.


Now it's time to inspire other Brazilians who want to go abroad (whatever who) through your own history and ideas.
